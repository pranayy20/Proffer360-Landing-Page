Features Deep Dive

Unified CRM & Database

Your Single Source of Truth for Community Data

Stop hunting for information across disconnected files. Proffer360's central database acts as your powerful CRM, securely managing all parent, child, and family data. Use the intelligent bulk-upload feature to import data from Excel/CSV in minutes, view detailed family profiles, and use the advanced export tool to create custom reports.

Dynamic Campaign Management

From Plan to Graduation, Seamlessly

Design, manage, and track every program with unparalleled flexibility. Whether it's a standard semester-long campaign or a multi-school "Special Event," our platform adapts to your needs. Utilize built-in task management with templates, track attendance with a few clicks, and manage participant enrollment or event registration all in one place.

PartnerConnect CRM

Strengthen Your Community Ties

Your partnerships are vital. PartnerConnect is a dedicated module to manage relationships with school districts, sponsors, and community organizations. Track key contacts, manage contracts with status and value, and view a complete history of associated campaigns and communications to nurture every partnership.

Intelligent Outreach Suite

Engage Every Family, Every Time

Connect with your community through targeted, effective communication. Our integrated suite combines broad Marketing campaigns (Email & SMS) with direct Communications. Create filtered audience lists, use beautiful templates, and maintain a full history of every message sent, ensuring no family falls through the cracks.

Powerful Analytics & Reporting

Turn Data into Actionable Insights

Understand your impact like never before. The reporting dashboard provides filterable, easy-to-understand analytics on everything from enrollment by school and family status to campaign attendance and geographic distribution of your families. Make informed decisions and showcase your success with compelling data.

Custom Certificates & Graduation Books

Celebrate Success, Effortlessly

Recognize participant achievement with professionally generated documents. Create and customize HTML templates for completion certificates or multi-page graduation books. The system can automatically determine eligibility based on your attendance criteria, saving you hours of administrative work.

Full Administrative Control

Customize the Platform to Your Needs

Proffer360 is built to fit your organization, not the other way around. Administrators have complete control over user roles, granular page-level permissions, and even campaign-specific access for staff. Configure your bulk-upload templates with custom fields and mappings to ensure data integrity from day one.
